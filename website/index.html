Questions

Asked before you join. Answered straight.

Is it free to join?

Yes. The Discord is completely free — no card, no trial, no catch. Just click the invite link and you're in.

What do I actually get in the community?

Access to daily pre-market bias, live intraday commentary, and real trade executions posted as they happen — all in the channel list you can see above. Everything is explained as it unfolds, not in hindsight.

Do you send signals?

No. What gets posted in #intraday-calls and #executions is real-time analysis and our actual trades — but you make your own decisions. The goal is that you understand the why, not just copy the what.

Is this for beginners?

Yes. You don't need experience to join. What you do need is a willingness to sit with the content, ask questions, and put in reps. The community is active and questions get answered.

What markets does the community focus on?

Primarily index futures (ES/NQ), major FX pairs, and gold during defined sessions. The setups transfer across instruments, but the focus stays tight rather than chasing everything.

Will I make money?

Unknown — and anyone who guarantees it is lying. What you get is a community with real results in #certificates-payouts and #profits, a structured way to learn, and people willing to help. What you do with that is up to you.

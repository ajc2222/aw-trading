Candle Variations

All dark-theme · pure CSS/SVG · uses existing site vars — pick one to replace index.html

Variation 1 Current
White→Blue Gradient
Up = white-to-blue · Down = grey ghost

The existing design. Issues: wicks blend into background, down candles barely visible, top of up-candles looks washed.

Variation 2
Solid Blue / Dimmed
Up = solid blue glow · Down = muted dim

Most on-brand. Solid blue up-candles pop against the dark card. Down candles use a dim grey so they don't compete. Wicks are clearly visible.

Variation 3
Trading Green / Red
Universal trader language

Most familiar to traders. Green/red is the universal convention. Every trader instantly reads the chart. Slightly breaks the blue brand palette.

Variation 4
Hollow / Filled (Dark)
Outline up · solid white down

Premium / editorial feel. Hollow white outline for bullish, solid white fill for bearish — the classic ICT chart style adapted for dark backgrounds. Sophisticated look.

Variation 5
Blue Glow Candles
Up = bright blue glow · Down = ghost

Most dramatic. Up candles have a bright inner glow that radiates off the card. Down candles are near-invisible ghosts. Very premium tech feel.

Variation 6
Teal / Amber
Unique palette · high contrast

Stands out from competition. Teal up-candles and amber down-candles are uncommon in trading UIs — instantly memorable. Both colours have great contrast on dark.

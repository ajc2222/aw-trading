## 1. YouTube SEO System

### Title Formula

```
[Primary keyword phrase] + [Specific outcome or hook]
```

**Rules:**
- 60 characters max (YouTube truncates after that in search)
- Lead with the keyword, not your name
- Outcome > description. "How I use ICT to catch reversals" > "ICT Trading Tutorial"
- Numbers and specifics outperform vague titles: "3-step" / "5 minute" / "$800 trade"

**Title templates to rotate:**
```
What is [concept] and Why Does It Actually Work?
How I [specific result] Using [concept] (Live Chart)
The [concept] Setup That Changed How I Trade
Why [common mistake] is Destroying Your Trades
[Number] Things Every ICT Trader Gets Wrong

```

### Reel Optimization

**The first 2 seconds determine everything.** If someone doesn't stop scrolling, watch time tanks, reach tanks.

**Hook formula:**
```
Text overlay (large, bold) + first spoken word within 1 second
```

Examples that stop the scroll:
- `"3 things retail traders get wrong"`
- `"This is what a liquidity sweep looks like"`
- `"I took $400 off this one setup"`
- `"ICT traders know this, you should too"`

**Reel structure:**
1. Hook (0–3 sec): text overlay + spoken line
2. Value delivery (4–50 sec): the actual content, fast-paced
3. CTA (last 5 sec): "link in bio for the free community"

**Editing notes:**
- Captions ON (auto-caption in CapCut) — 85% of Reels are watched muted
- Cut dead air ruthlessly — if you're not saying something, cut it
- Add text overlays for key terms as you say them
- Background music low (–20db) under voiceover

**Tier 2 — Medium (broader trading):**

**Tier 3 — Discovery (wide reach):**
`#trading` `#financialfreedom` `#investing`

**Do not use:** generic hashtags over 100M posts (#money, #success) — your content drowns.

---

### Engagement Window (most important habit)

**15 minutes before posting:**
Go engage on 10–15 posts in your niche (search #ICTtrading, #futurestrader). Leave real comments (2+ sentences, no emojis only). This warms the algorithm before your post goes out — it signals to Instagram that you're an active account in that niche.

**60 minutes after posting:**
Reply to every comment. Ask follow-up questions. More comments = more reach. The first hour is the window that matters.

---

### Story Strategy (daily habit — 5 min/day)

Stories keep your account "warm" and show up at the top of followers' feeds.

**Story types to rotate:**

| Type | Example | Frequency |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Chart question | "Would you have taken this trade?" + poll Yes/No | 2x/week |
| Behind the scenes | Screen recording of pre-market prep | 1x/week |
| YouTube CTA | "New video just dropped — link in bio" | Every upload day |
| Discord proof | Screenshot of a Discord education post | 1x/week |
| Soft indicator tease | Cropped indicator screenshot (Weeks 5–7) | Per week 5–7 plan |

**Key:** Use the poll sticker and question sticker often. These boost your Story reach significantly.

---

### Follower Growth Actions (10 min/day)

1. **Follow 15–20 accounts/day** in your niche: other ICT traders, prop firm accounts, trading educators. Instagram limits aggressive following — keep it under 200/day total.
2. **Comment on 10 posts** from accounts your target audience follows. Not "great post" — actual insight: "This is a great breakdown of the CISD — I've noticed the same pattern on NQ in the NY session."
3. **DM 3–5 people/week** who engaged on your posts. Not a pitch — acknowledge their comment: "Saw you asked about order blocks — I'm dropping a full breakdown on Friday." Builds real relationships.

## 3. Discord Conversion Bridge

### The Problem With "Link in Bio"

"Link in bio for the free community" is the default CTA and it's weak because it:
- Doesn't say why they should join *now*
- Doesn't describe what they get that they can't get from the video
- Doesn't create urgency

### The Sharp CTA Formula

Every video/Reel CTA should include **one specific thing they get in the Discord that they can't get anywhere else:**

```
"I post [specific thing] in the Discord every [frequency] — 
[link / link in bio] to join free."
```

**CTA variations to rotate:**

| Use When | CTA |
|----------|-----|
| YouTube video | "I break down my live trade setups the night before in the Discord — join free, link in the description." |
| Educational Reel | "I go deeper on this concept in the Discord's #free-education channel — all free, link in bio." |
| P&L Reel | "Discord members got the setup alert before this trade. Link in bio, it's free." |
| Trade breakdown | "I annotate a chart like this every week in the Discord and post it for free — link in bio." |
| Week 5+ (tease) | "Discord members are seeing the indicator first. Link in bio." |

**The rule:** Always name the specific thing. Not "free community" — "live setup annotations / pre-market breakdowns / indicator previews."

### The Free Value Flywheel

The Discord should feel like the *best* part of your content ecosystem — not just a community tab. Here's the loop:

```
YouTube video drops
  → Instagram Story points to the video
    → Video CTA sends people to Discord
      → Discord posts more depth (the "after" content)
        → Discord members share the content with their own network
          → New people discover the account
```

**Weekly Discord rhythm (already in execution plan, but the WHY):**
- 3x/week #free-education posts: These are what people tell their friends about. If someone reads a breakdown and learns something real, they share it. That's your organic growth engine.
- Cross-post every YouTube video in Discord with a "what's in this video + what I couldn't fit in" note. This rewards Discord members for being in the community — they get more than YouTube subscribers do.

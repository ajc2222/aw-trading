Questions

Asked before you join. Answered straight.

I'm a complete beginner. Is this for me?

Yes — Foundations assumes zero knowledge and builds from market structure up. What we require isn't experience; it's the willingness to journal, review, and follow rules. If you want to gamble, this isn't your place.

Do you send trade signals?

No. We trade live and explain every decision, but the goal is that within months you don't need us in the moment. Renting signals keeps you dependent. Owning a system sets you free.

What markets do you trade?

Primarily index futures (ES/NQ), major FX pairs, and gold — all during defined sessions. The setups transfer, but we master a small universe instead of chasing everything.

Will I make money?

Unknown, and anyone who guarantees it is lying to you. Trading involves substantial risk of loss. What we control is the quality of your education, your risk framework, and your accountability. The results are built by your reps.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Month to month, no lock-in, no hoops. If we're not earning your membership, you shouldn't be paying for it.

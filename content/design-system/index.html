Design System v1

One visual language, any domain.

Dark glass surfaces, one accent color, a mono/display type pairing, and a small set of motion rules — extracted from AW Trading's site and generalized so it drops into an education platform, a social app, or a dashboard without renaming a thing.

What does .reveal-gate do?

Blurs gated content and shows a lock overlay until a CTA click removes the `.locked` class and `inert` attribute — used for paywalled forms or previews.

How do I re-skin this for another brand?

Change --accent and the two --accent-glow/--accent-ink values in tokens.css. Every glow, badge, and focus ring derives from those three.

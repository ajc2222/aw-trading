## Rules

- **First 2 lines** appear in YouTube search before "Show more" — include the primary keyword naturally in line 1
- **Line 1** = one sentence summary, keyword-first (e.g. "ICT liquidity grabs explained with live NQ charts")
- **Bullet points** = specific takeaways, not vague descriptions
- **CTA** = rotate the Discord CTA based on what's in the video (see below)

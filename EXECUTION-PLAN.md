## Reference

### Mentorship Offer — full copy

**The AW Trading Founding Cohort** — get funded, stop blowing accounts, build a process you actually trust, with Will and AW in the room every week.

- **Live Trading Sessions** — 2×/week, on the charts in real time
- **Daily Access** — 30–45 min every day, direct answers from Will and AW
- **Trade Journal Review** — what you actually did, not what you meant to do
- **AW Indicator, included** — no separate subscription while enrolled
- **Private Cohort Channel** — nine other traders on the same 90 days

> "Show up to every live session and submit your journal for the full 90 days. If you do the work and don't think it was worth it, roll into the next cohort free — or get your money back."

**Scarcity:** 10 seats. Not ten-ish — Will and AW run every call personally, so that's the real ceiling.

### Rollover Offer — full copy

**AW Indicator — Early Access.** $39/mo, starts now. 100% of what's paid before Sept 1 credits toward the first Mentorship payment, up to the full $129.

- Real rollover, not a discount — the $129 price never moves
- First live revenue during the six otherwise-dark pre-launch weeks
- Pre-qualifies the applicant pool — a paying, active user beats a cold applicant
- Early Access members get first consideration for the 10 seats

**Implementation:** only the Indicator's gate flips early. Mentorship and prop firm codes stay exactly as planned.

### Leads Plan — channel detail

More before Better before New — nothing here is a new channel.

- **Warm Outreach** (currently off) — highest leverage, costs nothing, start first
- **Content** (under-used) — push to daily on both platforms before anything else
- **P&L Card** (idle asset) — brand it, nudge sharing, let it self-distribute
- **Referrals** (not engineered) — ask at peak result, same day, two-sided incentive
- **Giveaway** (running, underused) — push harder as recurring content, route entries into Discord

**Off the table for now:** paid ads, a third platform, prop firm affiliates — none of the channels above are maxed out yet.

### Giveaway Loop — full mechanic

Follow + join Discord = 1 entry. Each entrant gets a personal, tracked Discord invite link (free, native) — every referred join adds another entry, capped around 20.

- **Monthly** — 1–3 winners, a free month of the Indicator
- **Quarterly** — 1 winner, 6–12 months free, only when volume justifies it
- **Grand tier (rare)** — a guaranteed Founding Cohort application slot, never a free seat

Every prize is $0 cash cost — Indicator delivery is pure margin. The grand tier deliberately skips the queue instead of giving away a paid, capacity-limited seat, so the 10-seat scarcity stays real.
